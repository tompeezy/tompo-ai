Scope of Work

Get Notified LLC provides installation and configuration of smart monitoring devices including presence sensors, door/window sensors, water leak sensors, hubs, panic buttons, smoke detectors, and related equipment designed to send alerts and notifications.

Standard installation includes mounting sensors, connecting approved power sources, pairing devices to the hub, and confirming notifications are delivered to designated contacts.

Standard installation does NOT include: major electrical work, structural modifications, plumbing alterations, or drywall repair unless specifically agreed upon in writing.

Privacy & Data — Our Promise

✅ Your system runs locally on hardware inside your home.
✅ No cameras or microphones are installed unless specifically requested.
✅ Your data is never sent to the cloud or stored on Get Notified servers.
✅ The system is designed to work without an active internet connection.
✅ You own your system outright — no subscriptions required to operate it.
