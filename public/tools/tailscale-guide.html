Remote Support Setup

Tailscale Secure Access Guide

🔒

Your privacy is our foundation. Tailscale creates an encrypted private connection between your system and our support device only — it does not expose your home network, does not allow phone control, and does not give access to personal data. You can disconnect anytime.

To provide you with fast, secure remote support — without ever needing to visit your home for minor issues — we use Tailscale. It takes about 2 minutes to set up and works on both Android and iPhone. Follow the steps below for your device.

🤖
Android
Google Play Store
1
Install the App

Open Google Play Store → search Tailscale → tap Install

2
Open & Get Started

Launch Tailscale → tap Get Started

3
Allow VPN Permission

Android will ask permission to create a VPN connection — tap Allow

⚠️ This is normal and expected. Tailscale uses Android's VPN system to create an encrypted tunnel — it does not give us control of your phone.
4
Sign In

Choose any sign-in method: Google · Microsoft · GitHub · Apple

5
Turn On

Toggle Connect — you're on the secure network.

🍎
iPhone
Apple App Store
1
Install the App

Open App Store → search Tailscale → tap Get

2
Open & Get Started

Launch Tailscale → tap Get Started

3
Sign In

Choose any sign-in method: Apple · Google · Microsoft · GitHub

4
Allow VPN Configuration

iPhone will prompt: "Tailscale Would Like to Add VPN Configurations" — tap Allow

⚠️ Same as Android — this is expected. It's a secure tunnel, not phone access.
5
Connect

Toggle the VPN switch ON — you're connected.

Turn it off anytime from the app.
Your Privacy — Plain English

✅ Tailscale does not give Get Notified control of your phone.

✅ Tailscale does not give access to your photos, messages, or personal data.

✅ It only creates a secure connection to your Get Notified smart home hub — the small computer we installed in your home.

✅ You can disconnect and remove the app at any time with no impact on your system.

How to Disconnect

Open the Tailscale app → toggle Connect OFF. That's it. Remote access is immediately disabled until you turn it back on.
