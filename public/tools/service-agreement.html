Installation Contract

Description of Services

Get Notified LLC agrees to install smart monitoring and notification equipment as discussed with the customer — including but not limited to presence sensors, door/window sensors, water leak sensors, panic buttons, smoke detectors, hubs, and related devices.

Exact equipment and placement will be confirmed based on the property layout and customer preferences at the time of installation. A detailed quote will be provided prior to scheduling.

📋 This Service Agreement is paired with a separate Installation Liability Waiver — both documents must be signed before work begins.

Pricing & Scope Changes

The estimated total is based on the agreed scope of work. Final price may change if:

  • Additional equipment is requested by the customer
  • Installation conditions differ significantly from the initial assessment
  • Customer requests services outside the original scope

Any additional charges will be discussed and approved before work continues. Get Notified will never add charges without customer consent.

Scheduling & Cancellation

Customers must provide at least 24 hours notice to reschedule an installation appointment. Failure to provide notice may result in forfeiture of the deposit.

If the customer cancels after equipment has been purchased, the deposit will be retained to cover equipment, time, and travel costs. If equipment has not yet been ordered, a refund may be issued at Get Notified's discretion.

Customer Responsibilities

To ensure a smooth installation, customer agrees to:

  • Provide access to all installation areas on the scheduled date
  • Ensure active internet service and accessible power outlets are available
  • Provide Wi-Fi network name and password if required for device setup
  • Remove furniture, obstructions, or hazards that may interfere with installation
  • Have a decision-maker present or reachable during installation
What's Not Included

This agreement covers smart device installation only. The following are not included unless separately agreed upon in writing:

  • Licensed electrical or plumbing work
  • Structural modifications or drywall repair
  • Monthly monitoring service (available separately)
  • Ongoing technical support beyond initial setup
